This service is intended as a replacement for those who may not be able to afford lucrative services like Spotify, Rdio or Deezer and just want to be able to manage their music collections in the form of playlists.
Musicality is purely conceptually based. Certain inherent features are intentionally left out to draw the line between how a company would program their application to be suitable for production and how an initial prototype would look.
Built on top of core technologies such as node.js and Angular.js.
